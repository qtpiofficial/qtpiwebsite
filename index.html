ABOUT THE COMPANY

Hi! We love robots so much that we design and manufacture most of the parts inhouse so that you can build your imagination on our platform. You can make cute toys or build miniatures of real world automation without reinventing the wheel. But You will learn the nut and bolts of robotics when you play with QtPi. Robotics is a both art and science. We have solved the basics of arts and science for you. Now its your turn. Be the creator!!! Go Innovate!!! With a mission to reach everyone across the socio-economic spectrum, we at QtPi have produced a beautiful ensemble of build-and-learn robot sets that are not only cost-effective, but also amazingly modular - flexible enough to create multiple robots by combining them differently.
You will be amazed, more than amused about your innate potential once you start playing with QtPi. Just as the alphabets becomes words and build amazing stories, just as seven notes combine in a million ways to create music, our kit has been thoughtfully designed to provide you the basics with the best-in-class components and modules to allow you to build stories of your own. At the core, QTPi runs on the revolutionary chipset, Arduino, that best suits for learning purposes. Science is continuously evolving.
At the ebb of this expansion is technological innovation in robotics. Some are perceivable, some are not. In an all-consuming, all digital world that would in future, be increasingly dependent, learning about how robots and automation work is truly necessary for everyone, especially our children. As the Internet is spreading wings, and getting all the tangible, and intangible stuff from our everyday lives connected to it, in order to make everything most useful and efficient, at the core lies robotics and artificial intelligence. Touch, see, and feel the learning.
Rote learning fails to kindle the imagination of our children often, and the lessons are forgotten. QtPi robot kits are built exactly to bridge this gap. What you see is what you get, literally! It will rekindle the basics you learn and mostly it will trigger curiosity to learn the basics. You will start appreciating everything around you when you understand the basics of physics.
